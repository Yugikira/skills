# Price Informativeness

## Definition
The degree to which the price system reveals the true signal θ to uninformed traders. Measured by squared correlation coefficient ρ_θ² = 1/(1+m).

## Mathematical Representation
ρ_θ² = 1/(1+m), where m = (aσ_ε²/λ)² × σ_x²/σ_θ²

## Determinants
- **Higher** when: n↑, c↓, a↓ (from Equation 19b)
- **Unchanged** when: σ_x² or σ_u² changes (λ adjusts to offset)

## Role in Model
Determines how much informed traders' private information is transmitted to uninformed traders through price observations.
