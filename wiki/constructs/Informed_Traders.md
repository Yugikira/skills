# Informed Traders

## Definition
Traders who pay cost c to observe the signal θ (the observable component of risky asset return u = θ + ε).

## Mathematical Representation
Demand function: X_I(P,θ) = (θ - RP)/(aσ_ε²)

## Role in Model
- Observe θ, trade based on private information
- Gain from trading with uninformed at "mispriced" levels
- As λ↑, information advantage diminishes (more transmitted via price)
