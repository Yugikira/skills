# Proportion Informed

## Definition
The fraction λ of traders who pay cost c to observe the signal θ (informed traders).

## Mathematical Representation
λ ∈ [0, 1], endogenously determined by equilibrium condition γ(λ) = 1

## Role in Model
- Higher λ → more informative price system (but informed utility falls relative to uninformed)
- Equilibrium λ balances information value against cost
- λ adjusts to offset noise changes, keeping informativeness constant
