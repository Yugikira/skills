# Uninformed Traders

## Definition
Traders who only observe the price P, infer information about θ from the price system with rational expectations.

## Mathematical Representation
Demand function: X_U(P;P*) = (E[u*|P* = P] - RP)/(aVar[u*|P* = P])

## Role in Model
- Learn relationship between price and returns over time
- Cannot distinguish price movements from θ vs x (supply noise)
- Free-ride on informed traders' information via price observation
