# Information Quality

## Definition
Ratio of observable signal variance to noise variance, n = σ_θ²/σ_ε². Higher n means more precise private information.

## Mathematical Representation
n = σ_θ²/σ_ε², where σ_θ² = Var(θ), σ_ε² = Var(ε)

## Role in Model
- Higher n → more informative price system (ρ_θ² rises)
- n/(1+n) = squared correlation between θ and u
- Effect on λ ambiguous: benefits both informed and uninformed
